"Your Discovery is dangerous," the man said with a cold Judgment. "We have a Responsibility to control this Information for the sake of Prosperity." Ray disagreed, believing in the Principle of Liberty and Democracy. "Is this about Justice, or just your personal Profit?" Ray asked, his voice full of Bravery. He knew the Relationship between power and Honesty was often Fragile. The Management of this secret required a Professional Approach, not a hidden Mission. "You have no Permission to be here," the man growled, the Pressure in the room rising. Ray checked his Electronic device for an Alternative exit. He noticed the Structure of the wall was Unstable due to Humidity. "My Objective is to protect my father’s Heritage," Ray declared. He realized that Education and Knowledge were his only Weapons against such a Terrible Situation. "Finally," Ray whispered as he found a hidden Passage, "the Revolution of the truth begins."
