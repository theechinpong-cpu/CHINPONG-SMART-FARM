Hello everyone! My name is [Student Name]. Today, I am very happy to talk about my life and my family.

On my free time, I like to play in the green park. I can run, jump, and skip on the grass. Look! There is a small black cat sitting on a mat. It is so cute!

When it is lunch time, my mom and dad bring a big box. We have sweet grapes, red apples, and fresh cake. The sun is bright, and the breeze is cool.

I love reading books under the tall tree. My sister likes to sing a song. We smile and laugh all day.

Learning Phonics helps me speak clearly. I love my family and I love English! Thank you very much!
