แบบทดสอบ Present Simple Tense (ระดับเข้มข้น)

สำหรับนักเรียน ม.1 โรงเรียนนานาชาติ

1. Rarely _____ he miss a class, even when he feels unwell.

คำอธิบาย: Negative adverb 'Rarely' triggers inversion; requires auxiliary 'does'.

2. The committee _____ every Tuesday to discuss the curriculum updates.

คำอธิบาย: Collective noun 'committee' acts as a singular unit here.

3. Neither the students nor the teacher _____ the answer to this complex problem.

คำอธิบาย: In 'Neither/nor' structures, verb agrees with the closer subject (teacher).

4. Water _____ at 100 degrees Celsius under standard atmospheric pressure.

คำอธิบาย: General scientific truth uses Present Simple.

5. Everyone _____ that the school trip is scheduled for next Friday.

คำอธิบาย: 'Everyone' is singular, requiring singular verb form.

6. Unless you _____ harder, you won't pass the final examination.

คำอธิบาย: First conditional: 'unless' clause takes Present Simple.

7. My father, along with his colleagues, _____ to the office every morning.

คำอธิบาย: Prepositional phrase 'along with' does not change the singular subject 'father'.

8. Physics _____ a challenging subject for most secondary students.

คำอธิบาย: Academic subjects ending in -s are grammatically singular.

9. The news _____ always surprising to those who don't follow the updates.

คำอธิบาย: 'News' is an uncountable noun, treated as singular.

10. If it _____ tomorrow, we will hold the assembly in the indoor gym.

คำอธิบาย: First conditional: 'if' clause takes Present Simple.

11. Mathematics _____ my favorite subject because of its logical structure.

คำอธิบาย: 'Mathematics' is singular.

12. The local library _____ at 6 PM sharp on weekdays.

คำอธิบาย: Fixed schedules use Present Simple.

13. Nobody _____ to be interrupted while working on a project.

คำอธิบาย: 'Nobody' is an indefinite pronoun, takes singular verb.

14. She _____ believe in ghosts despite her friends' stories.

คำอธิบาย: Stative verb 'believe' requires 'doesn't' for negative.

15. How often _____ the school bus arrive at your stop?

คำอธิบาย: Subject 'school bus' is singular, requires 'does'.

16. The flock of birds _____ over the school field every afternoon.

คำอธิบาย: 'Flock' is the head noun (singular).

17. He _____ English, French, and a bit of Mandarin fluently.

คำอธิบาย: Singular subject 'He' requires -s.

18. The data _____ that students who read daily perform better.

คำอธิบาย: In modern academic context, 'data' is often treated as plural, but 'shows' is common for the collective finding.

19. Each of the participants _____ a certificate after the workshop.

คำอธิบาย: 'Each' takes a singular verb.

20. What time _____ your classes usually begin?

คำอธิบาย: Plural subject 'classes' requires 'do'.

21. The smell of fresh flowers _____ the entire corridor.

คำอธิบาย: Head noun 'smell' is singular.

22. My best friend _____ a collection of rare coins from around the world.

คำอธิบาย: Singular subject 'friend' requires -s.

23. Either the teacher or the students _____ the classroom door.

คำอธิบาย: Verb agrees with the closer subject 'students' (plural).

24. She _____ coffee because it makes her feel jittery.

คำอธิบาย: Singular subject 'she' requires -s.

25. Always _____ the instructions carefully before starting the exam.

คำอธิบาย: Imperative sentence uses base verb.
