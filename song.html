🎶 The Great Adventure

Vocabulary Practice Song for Kids

[Verse 1]

Wake up! Put on your Shirt and Pants.
A New Dress, Skirt, let’s start to dance.
Put on your Sock, put on your Shoe.
Wear a Hat, a Coat, and a Belt too.
My Ring is bright, let’s go to the Door.
Look at the Window, walk on the Floor.
The Wall is tall, the Roof is high.
Turn on the Lamp, look at the sky!

[Chorus]

Oh! The Earth, the Sun, the Moon, the Star.
The Cloud, the Rain, the Storm is far.
The Wind is cool, the Fire is hot.
We love this world, we love it a lot!
(Sing it Loud! Don't be Quiet!)

[Verse 2]

Look in the Mirror, use the Soap.
A Soft Towel gives us hope.
Get a Spoon, a Fork, and a Knife.
Plate, Bowl, and Glass for a good life.
Are you Angry, Brave, or Scared today?
Are you Tired, Thirsty, or want to play?
Is it Dirty or Clean? Is it Hard or Soft?
Is it Heavy or Light? Lift it aloft!

[Chorus]

Oh! The Earth, the Sun, the Moon, the Star.
The Cloud, the Rain, the Storm is far.
The Wind is cool, the Fire is hot.
We love this world, we love it a lot!

[Bridge]

The box is Full, the box is Empty.
The Rich and Poor have friends a plenty.
Drink the Water, walk on the Land.
Go to the Sea, the Mountain is grand!
Explore the Cave, turn Left and Right.
Go Up and Down, it's such a sight!
Inside, Outside, Between, Behind.
Under and Above, what can you find?

[Verse 3]

Eat a Fruit, see an Animal run.
Every Person is having fun.
Use your Money, write on Paper.
See a Picture, be a world-shaper.
Listen to Music, sing a Song.
A lovely Sound, we sing along.
Look at the Color, see the Shape.
Square, Circle, Triangle, no escape!
A Heart for you, on the Beach so wide.
An Island, a River, a Lake to hide.

[Verse 4]

In the Forest, Garden, or the Farm.
The Park, the City, the Town has charm.
Apple, Banana, Orange, and Grape.
Mango, Lemon, in every shape.
Candy, Bread, Butter, and Cheese.
Pass them to me, if you please!
A Doctor, Nurse, Police on the street.
Pilot, Chef, Teacher we meet.
Farmer, Worker, Driver, and Singer.
On every Table, we point a finger!

[Verse 5]

Sit on a Chair, at your Desk so neat.
On the Bed or Sofa, take a seat.
Put it in a Box, drink from a Cup.
A Bottle, a Key, pick them Up.
Check your Watch, is it Fast or Slow?
Is it Small or Large? Do you know?
Short or Tall, Young or Old.
We are Happy, we are gold!

[Outro]

No more Sad, only Good and Nice.
Cool, Warm, Cold, or Hot as ice.
Everything is Good, nothing is Bad.
The best adventure we've ever had!
(Yeah! Happy and New!)
