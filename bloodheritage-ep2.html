Ray arrived at the Clinic, his breath heavy with a Headache. Inside, the Engineer was busy Cooking something that smelled like Liquid metal. "I Always knew they would find you," the old man said, looking Nervous. He handed Ray a Magnet device and a Camera to capture the Quality of the ruins. Suddenly, the Signal on his Tablet went dead. A Soldier was outside, carrying a Hammer to break the lock. Ray jumped through the window, his Muscle aching as he hit the ground. He ran toward the harbor in the Autumn rain, feeling Lonely but Proud of his choice. At the docks, a Sailor was waiting by a Helicopter near the Ocean. "To survive, you need Vitamin C and a sharp Telescope," she joked, trying to keep him Healthy and calm. They flew over a Valley and a dormant Volcano as the Weather cleared. Ray looked at a Rainbow and tried to Imagine the Diamond treasure his father had mentioned. "The Gravity of this secret is Heavy," the woman said, checking the Height. "But if you are Honest, the Internet of the ancients will open for you." They were heading to a place where the water was Shallow, but the secrets were Deep.
