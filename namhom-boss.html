🏐 Volleyball Adventure! 🏐

Scene: At the Volleyball Court
Namhom: Hello, Boss! Today is very Hot. Look at the Sun in the Earth sky. There is no Cloud.
Boss: Yes, Namhom. I am ready. Look at my New Shirt and Pants. They are Clean and Light. I can run Fast.
Namhom: Good! Check your Shoe and Sock too. I wear a Hat for the sun. I see a Farmer and a Worker near the Garden and Farm.
Boss: Wow! Many people are here. There is a Doctor, Nurse, Police, Pilot, and Chef. I see a Teacher and a Singer on the Large Chair and Sofa.
Namhom: I am excited! I feel Brave but a little Scared. The other team is very Tall.
Boss: Don't be Sad. Be Happy! Look, the Coach puts a Box and a Bottle Under the Table near that Desk. If you are Thirsty, drink some Water.
Namhom: Thank you. Boss, look at the Mirror on the Wall. I need to check my Belt. Oh! My Ring is on the Floor.
Boss: Put it in the Cup or Bowl. People are Loud now. I cannot hear any Sound. Namhom, move to the Left. I will put the Apple, Banana, and Orange on the Plate.
Namhom: Okay. I have Grape, Mango, and Lemon too. We have Bread, Butter, and Cheese if our tummies are Empty.
Boss: Look! People hold Picture of Heart and Triangle. I see balloons with Circle and Square Shape.
Namhom: The game starts! Boss, hit the ball to the Right and hit it Down. I will jump Up.
Boss: Okay! The ball is Hard and Heavy, but we can do it. We will be Rich with points, not Poor!
After the game
Namhom: We win! I am Tired but happy. Look at the Moon and Star. It is Cold and Cool now.
Boss: Yes. The Wind is strong. Maybe there is Rain or a Storm. Namhom, wear your Coat quickly.
Namhom: Let's go to the room. I want to use Soap and a Soft Towel. Our room has a Window to see the Sea, Beach, and Island.
Boss: We have a bright Lamp and a soft Bed. I want to listen to Music and a Song.
Namhom: Use the Key for the Door. Boss, did you take the Spoon, Fork, and Knife? And don't forget your Watch Between the bottle and the vase.
Boss: Yes. We are Inside the building. The Roof is strong. Outside is Dirty, but here is Clean.
Namhom: Goodnight, Boss. Tomorrow, let's go to the Forest, Lake, or Mountain. Or we go to the City and Town?
Boss: Everywhere! But now, I want a Candy. Be Quiet, I will eat it now!
