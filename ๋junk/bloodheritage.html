Blood Heritage: The Zero Point

Chapter 1: The Signal from Yesterday

The air in London during Winter was freezing, biting deep into the Bone. Yesterday had been a typical, Bored day for Ray, a Young 17-year-old student. But today, his History was about to collide with the Future. Ray sat in the silent Library, staring at his Laptop. He was supposed to be studying Math and Geography, but his Brain was focused on a mysterious Email he had just received. There was no sender name, only a complex Password prompt. Ray moved the Mouse with trembling fingers. Suddenly, the Battery icon flashed—it was at Zero percent! "No! Where is the Charger?" he muttered Loudly, earning a sharp look from the librarian. He moved Quickly yet Quietly to plug it in. As soon as the Screen flickered back to life, a strange Object appeared. It was a digital map showing a hidden coordinate in a deep Jungle, a place not found on any modern Calendar. Ray felt Excited, his Heart racing. He began to Notice geometric patterns—a Rectangle, a Sphere, and a glowing Cube—hidden within the image. This wasn't just a Game anymore. The message read: "Be Brave and Careful. The truth of your Blood heritage is buried where Science meets myth." Ray didn't waste time. He packed his Hiking bag with a Flashlight, a Compass, and some basic Medicine in case he got Sick or developed a Fever. He knew this Journey would be dangerous. He needed to find an old Engineer who ran a small Clinic for advanced gadgets on the outskirts of the city. As he stepped outside, the Weather turned harsh. He pulled his school Uniform tight against the wind. Sometimes, life requires a perfect Balance between caution and courage. He looked up at an Airplane disappearing into clouds heavy with Lightning. "I have to find the Reason they chose me," Ray whispered. Suddenly, a new Message popped up on his Tablet: "They are coming for you. Go to the airport now!" The Adventure of a lifetime had officially begun.
