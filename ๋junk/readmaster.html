In a Big Castle Above the Cloudy Forest, an Actor and an Actress were Able to start an Adventure About a Golden Apple. They had to walk Across the Bridge to the Airport.

The Action began in the Afternoon. The Adult gave Advice to the Brave Child. "Don't be Afraid of the Giant Bat Against the wall," he said.

The Baby was Alive and Beautiful. Any Bird could Arrive. The Band played Art music. They Ask for Barbecue and Beef near the Bank.
