The Exploration of the island was officially over, and Ray returned to a life of Celebrity and Recognition. He spent his days in a Private Laboratory, focused on the Maintenance of the data he had saved. "This is my Contribution to Knowledge," Ray said during a global Committee Conversation. He felt a sense of Satisfaction, knowing he had acted with Honesty and Discipline. However, the Psychology of the world was changing; many had a Perspective of fear toward the new Technology. "The Reliability of our System is a Priority," Ray wrote in his final Report. He received a Recommendation for a Professional Position at the University of Future Sciences. But his Curiosity was still active; a Legendary Mystery about his father’s Biography remained. Ray looked at a Picture of a distant Gigantic Mountain on his Online Platform. "The Challenge is never over," Ray whispered, feeling Confidence in his Potential. He knew the Difference between Failure and Success was a Process of constant Improvement. "Everything has a Purpose," he concluded, looking at the Horizon. The Vibration of a new Message on his phone brought a Possibility of a new Adventure. Ray smiled, ready for the Evolution of his own Destination. The History of the future was just beginning.
